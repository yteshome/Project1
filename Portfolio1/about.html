The State of Michigan is blessed with the riches of unspoiled nature: the nation's longest freshwater coastline, lakes that feel like oceans, golden beaches, an abundance of fresh produce straight from the farm, glorious sunrises and sunsets and endless opportunities for recreation.

As the warm sun rises above the horizon, a new day calls us outside to the enchanting waters of Pure Michigan. We call it “The Lake Effect.” With long days, comfortable temperatures and the nation’s longest freshwater coastline, Michigan is America’s picture-perfect summer destination. The state’s more than 11,000 lakes set a scene of peaceful tranquility while also providing a breathtaking backdrop to an endless list of activities for everyone.
